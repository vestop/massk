MASSK STORY

THE IDEA OF THE SETZI SADDLE DID NOT COME OUT OF THE BLUE, BUT IS THE RESULT OF YEARS OF STUDY AND TESTING MADE BY ANTONIO SARDO (FOUNDER AND CEO OF SETZI SRL).

ABOUT US

Among the many saddles he tried out, Antonio rediscovered the old Sardinian saddle used by the shepherds when carrying milk churns over uneven stony roads and observed that, while it reduced problems for the horse, it also provided adequate comfort and stability for the rider.

Setzi is already well known and appreciated in the Endurance world. Research is ongoing along with Antonio`s son, Marco Sardo (professional and international endurance trainer in the UAE) are both involved in regular improvements on the technical aspects of the saddle.
